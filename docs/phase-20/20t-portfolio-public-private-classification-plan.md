## Purpose

20T classifies the JB3Ai product and service ecosystem before any broader portfolio rollout.

The goal is to prevent unsafe overexposure, avoid unsupported claims, and create a controlled public/private roadmap. The classification separates public product/app cards, public service offerings, internal systems, future public candidates that depend on OS3/dashboard readiness, and products excluded from the current public rollout.

This keeps the portfolio architecture disciplined: IsikoloAI can remain the public-good education anchor while the wider JB3Ai ecosystem is reviewed, staged, and published only through dedicated future milestones.

## Integration Rule

20T does not integrate products into the public website.

20T only classifies what may become public, what stays private, what belongs under services, and what requires future review.

No UI, route, app ID, `/os`, payment, contact/social, asset registry, public asset, PDF, raw media, or visible homepage change occurs in 20T.

Known asset context:

The founder has icons, cutsheets, brochures, copy text, images, and some videos for multiple applications. These materials are available for future review but remain intake-only by default. 20T must not move, publish, link, optimise, register, or commit any of these assets. It may only document that they exist and define the future review pathway.
