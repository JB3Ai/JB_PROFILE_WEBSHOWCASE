# Phase 20A: Selective Website Integration Planning

## Review Status

- Drafted in 20A
- Docs-only planning
- Pending human review
- Not published
- Not wired into website

## Purpose

This plan converts the Phase 19 closeout material into a controlled future integration roadmap for the public website.

Phase 19 produced the safe source layer, extract layer, review layer, consolidated index, and closeout guardrails. Phase 20A decides what may be considered for later public website integration, what must remain permission-gated, and what should be handled in smaller follow-on milestones.

## Integration Rule

No public website integration happens in 20A. This plan only decides what may be integrated later.

No source files, routes, app IDs, components, registry entries, public copy, image assets, or `public/` files should be changed by this milestone.

## Not Approved for Direct Integration Yet

The following items must not be integrated directly into the public website:

- Direct testimonial quotes.
- Named testimonials without permission.
- GTR WebP exports until final image selection.
- Raw candidate media.
- Awards language that implies winning unless verified.
- Current iMED service, accreditation, licensing, regulatory, or test-availability claims.
- Sensitive Trust In Hope resident or recovery details.
- Legal, family, court, evidence, financial, or private case material.
- Confidential lab, client, patient, or resident data.
- Any GTR³ arrival claim.
