## Purpose

20G defines the rules and readiness requirements for future Isikolo sponsorship links before any money buttons go live.

This is not a payment integration. It is a planning pass for support terms, payment-link readiness, qualifying supporter rules, GTR³ limited-edition reward terms, shipping, delay/refund wording, and supporter tracking requirements.

## Public Positioning

- Mission-led, not hardship-led.
- Sponsor Isikolo, not donate to me.
- Founding support, not emergency support.
- Impact funding, not pity.
- Core public line: Isikolo is built. Now help keep it free.

## Approved Public Copy Direction

Isikolo is built.

It is a free AI-powered education app created to support South African learners with schoolwork, study help, revision, explanations, and practical learning support.

The mission is simple: keep Isikolo free for learners in South Africa.

Founding support helps fund AI credits, hosting, platform infrastructure, curriculum content, local language support, learner access, and continued development.

International supporters, sponsors, and partners can help make free access possible.

Isikolo is built. Now help keep it free.

## Qualifying Supporter Rules

The following rules must be decided before live sponsorship links are added:

- Minimum support amount for each tier.
- Qualifying date range.
- One-time versus recurring support eligibility.
- Whether multiple payments accumulate toward a higher tier.
- Supporter name and email capture.
- Whether anonymous supporters qualify for rewards.
- Proof of payment handling.
- Geographic limitations.
- Reward eligibility rules.
- Whether sponsor names can be displayed publicly.
- How duplicate payments or failed payments are handled.

## GTR³ Limited-Edition Reward Terms

- Production timelines are estimates until printing or digital release terms are confirmed.
- The current release target may change.

## Delay / Refund / Non-Release Wording

Draft wording for review only:

Support helps fund independent education technology, learner access, AI credits, hosting, content, and platform development. Any limited-edition GTR³ book reward remains subject to final publication, production, availability, and delivery logistics. Release and delivery timelines are estimates and may change.

If the book reward is delayed, supporters will be updated using the contact details provided at the time of support. If the reward cannot be produced or delivered, the final refund or alternative-reward policy must be handled under the approved supporter terms published before live payment links are launched.

Final wording must be reviewed and approved before any live payment links are added.
