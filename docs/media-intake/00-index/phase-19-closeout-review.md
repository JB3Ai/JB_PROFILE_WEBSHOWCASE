## Do Not Integrate Yet

These items must not be integrated into the live website until a future milestone explicitly approves them:

- Raw media from `docs/media-intake/*-candidates/`.
- Any files under `docs/media-intake/06-book-gtr3/` that are still raw originals.
- Direct testimonial quotes.
- Named recommender/client material.
- Private family, legal, court, evidence, financial, or private case material.
- Confidential lab, patient, resident, or client details.
- GTR image exports without final visual selection and public-use approval.
- Any language implying iMED currently operates or offers services.
- Any language implying GTR³ already exists or has been obtained.
