## Testimonial Use Policy

- Direct quotes require source verification.
- Named quotes require permission confirmation before publication.
- Anonymous or paraphrased summaries are safer for early public use.
- Sensitive recovery, legal, family, financial, medical, laboratory, and case
  details must be excluded.
- Private resident, client, patient, or vulnerable-person details must be
  excluded.
- iMED must remain historical and must not read as a current operating claim.
- GTR³ remains symbolic and aspirational.
