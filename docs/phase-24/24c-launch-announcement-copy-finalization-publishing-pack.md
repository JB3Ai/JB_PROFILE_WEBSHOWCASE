## Messaging Principles

Use these principles for launch communication:

- Keep the tone founder-led, professional, controlled, and grounded.
- Present the site as a clear public profile layer, not as a full product launch for every internal idea.
- Emphasize clarity, systems, support pathways, and launch discipline.
- Mention only approved public products/resources.
- Avoid unsupported adoption, awards, licensing, school, government, medical, legal, financial, surveillance, intelligence, or operational claims.
- Keep held/review products private until separately approved.
- Keep JB³Ai and OS³ brand-facing naming intact.
